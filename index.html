  • Instructions
    1. Select a type of circuit to solve for from the list above.
    2. Click the "generate a circuit" button to get a circuit to solve for. You can hit this button as much as you wish to generate different variants.
    3. Solve for the missing values and enter your answers into the spaces provided.
    4. Click the "check answers" button to will highlight answers based on how correct they are:
      • Green: 100% correct
      • Yellow: Correct within 0.2%
      • Orange: Correct within 1%
      • Red: Incorrect
    5. If you get stuck you can click "show answers" to see what the correct answers are. This will require you to generate a new circuit before you can continue to work.
    6. Repeat these steps to continue to practice.
